The cities we cover

We decided to start offering our services in Frankfurt because, after conducting a research, we discover that this country has a really disproportionate number of spots in kindergarten with respect to the total number of kids. Indeed about 380.000 kids were not able to enter in the public childcare infrastructures.

Companies we work with

    Commerzbank

    Commerzbank is a major German bank with a strong presence in Europe, particularly in corporate banking. Headquartered in Frankfurt, it provides a wide range of financial services to both corporate and private clients, emphasizing innovation in digital banking and sustainable finance solutions.

    Siemens

    Siemens AG is a global leader in engineering and technology solutions. With a strong focus on electrification, automation and digitalization, Siemens operates in sectors such as energy, healthcare, and industrial manufacturing, aiming to drive sustainable and digital transformations worldwide.

    Merck Group

    Merck is one of the world's oldest science and technology companies, specializing in healthcare, life sciences, and performance materials. Headquartered in Darmstadt, Merck focuses on innovative research, especially in pharmaceuticals, laboratory tools, and high-tech materials used in electronics.

ESG responsability

Focus on working mothers

Our onsite childcare service directly supports companies’ ESG and Social Responsibility goals by empowering working parents, especially mothers, with young children and promoting a family-friendly workplace culture

Through accessible, high-quality childcare within the workplace, we create an environment where mothers can excel professionally without compromising their family responsibilities, thereby enhancing social equity and gender inclusivity
